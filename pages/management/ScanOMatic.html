How to use this scanner
  1. Select the appropriate status from the dropdown menu.
  2. If the status is "Shipped to Partner", you can optionally enter a tracking number.
  3. Scan the SP number using the camera button or type it in manually.
  4. Click on the camera icon to activate your device's camera for scanning.
  5. Once scanned, the item will be recorded and appear in the scanned items list.

Note: You can also use a physical barcode scanner connected to your device.
