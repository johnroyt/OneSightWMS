How to Use the Scanner

  1. Select a status from the dropdown menu.
  2. If you selected "Shipped to Partner", enter or scan a tracking number.
  3. Scan SP numbers using the camera button or a barcode scanner.
  4. Each successful scan will update the record in the system.
  5. You can also enter SP numbers manually in the field at the bottom.
  6. If you selected "Shipped to Partner", you can print a packing slip once you've scanned items.
